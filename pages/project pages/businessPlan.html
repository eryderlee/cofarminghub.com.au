Mission Statement

The journey to a greener West is as noble as it is trying. WynTree Friends, with the expertise of Dr. Billey Tang and innovative aquaponic solutions, aims to revegetate Melbourne's western suburbs, which have the lowest tree cover. Our mission is to enhance sustainability and community well-being through urban gardening.

Our Products

  • Aquaponic Kits: Combining aquaculture and hydroponics, these kits are designed for ease of use, allowing individuals to grow fresh produce while minimizing space and resource usage.
  • Terrarium Kits: These kits enable individuals to create and maintain miniature ecosystems in their homes or offices, promoting biodiversity education and mindfulness.

Market Segments

  • Schools: Engaging students with hands-on sustainable agriculture programs.
  • Councils: Partnering with local councils to support urban greening and community gardens.
  • Aged Care Facilities: Offering therapeutic gardening activities to enhance residents' well-being.
  • Households: Providing urban households with convenient home gardening solutions.

Operations and Production

  • Aquaponics: Utilizing sustainable methods to produce multiple agricultural products from single nutrient sources.
  • Terrariums: Designed for manageability and space efficiency, promoting home-grown fresh produce.

Strengths and Opportunities

  • Aquaponics: High production volume, sustainable practices, and local employment opportunities.
  • Terrariums: Manageable, space-efficient, and promotes self-sufficiency in fresh produce.

Challenges and Mitigation

Aquaponics

  • High initial cost, climate adaptation, and education gaps.

Terrariums

  • Equipment needs and limited plant variety.

Risk Management

Market Risks

  • Product diversification and customer education campaigns.

Operational Risks

  • Regular maintenance, strong supplier relationships, and workforce training.

Financial Risks

  • Robust financial planning, cost control, and risk hedging.

Financial Plan

Our financial projections demonstrate significant growth potential, driven by strategic investments and market expansion. By providing cost-effective solutions and maintaining financial prudence, WynTree Friends aims for long-term success in the sustainable agriculture market.
