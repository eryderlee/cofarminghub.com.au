About Us

Co-Farming Hub empowers micro-entrepreneurs—especially NDIS participants and others facing employment barriers—to launch eco-friendly home-based e-commerce businesses.

Through our digital-franchise platform, you get low-barrier access to training, mentorship, tools and a curated product suite to build a sustainable livelihood.

Our Story

In early 2025, our founder Raymond Loh teamed up with visionary mentors to solve a simple problem: how to turn employment barriers into green business opportunities.

The result was the Wyndham NDIS Pilot—a digital-franchise trial that onboarded 20 participants, combining co-farming tech with carbon-tracking tools to prove the model’s impact.
